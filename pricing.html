Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

For Lead Research projects we simply charge per contact. There are no subscriptions. Each lead list order minimum is $100 per list.

For Managed Outreach engagements it varies on a case by case basis. Please contact us to chat about your needs and we can provide a free quote.

How quickly can I get up and running with this?

Getting setup with us can happen same day.

Where are your agents located?

Our agents are located all across the United States within every time zone across the continental US. We also have a number of agents within our location in the Philippines to help with data extraction.

Do you record all of your calls?

Due to the variance of state laws regarding the recording of telephone conversations, we can only record calls that take place in One-Party Consent states legally.

Are you HIPPA compliant?

We do have HIPPA compliant capabilities. Please contact our team to learn more.

Do you offer a free trial?

We do not offer free trials due to the human nature of our work. You can get started with us a low as $100 through buying a lead list.

For your managed outreach package, how many leads can you generate for us each month?

This depends on many factors like your product, web presence, the market, your target, etc.... We've set 50+ meetings per month for clients all the way down to 0. During our kick off call we can discuss options and strategies to ensure the highest meeting count possible.
